Termination Process

Objective: The objective of termination process is to store the employee's resignation, stop attendance & salary processes; to initiate exit-clearance process and lastly the full and finalization process. The date of leaving entered in this form is taken as "project income till date" in employee master and not DOL in employee master. This process is treated as a temporary area as termination can also be cancelled. Date of Resignation, Date of leaving and Settlement Date (if available) entered here is picked at the time of full and finalization and then on to employee master. Rehireable check box is an important feature as it prompts at the time a new employee is added with same description like same name, father name, PAN Etc.
